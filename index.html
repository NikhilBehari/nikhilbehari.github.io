Nikhil Behari

I'm a graduate student in the Camera Culture group at the MIT Media Lab, advised by Professor Ramesh Raskar. My research interests include computational imaging, 3D scene understanding, and multi-agent decision-making, with a focus on automating imaging system design for 3D perception in human and planetary health. My research is supported by the NASA Space Technology Graduate Research Fellowship, under which I am advised by Dr. Corey Ippolito.

I received my B.A. in Computer Science and Statistics from Harvard University in 2022. During my undergrad, I was a Research Fellow at the Harvard Center for Research on Computation and Society advised by Prof. Milind Tambe. I am also a Non-Resident Tutor for Harvard's Mather House, where I lead the Higher Education advising team.
